Hiring / Onboarding

Diploma / GED / OSHA List

What: Confirms whether each technician has submitted OSHA 10/30 certification and a Diploma or GED.

Why: Required to verify eligibility for NEBB exam certification and ensure compliance.

When: Requested immediately after the offer letter is sent via Indeed messages.

📄 Diploma_GED_OSHA List.xlsx
Hiring / Onboarding

OSHA 10 & 30 Cards – Certificates

What: SharePoint location storing OSHA 10 and 30 cards/certificates, organized by region.

Why: Centralized official record of OSHA certifications — stores actual documents, not just Yes/No status.

When: Uploaded as soon as documents are received via Indeed message or email.

🗂️ SharePoint: OSHA 10 & 30 Cards – All Documents
Hiring / Onboarding

Tech Education Certs (SharePoint)

What: SharePoint location for technicians' education documents (Diploma, GED, transcripts). Files saved as LastName, FirstName.

Why: Education records required for future technician certification.

When: Requested immediately after the offer letter — same message as OSHA request.

Responsibilities & Workflows

Step-by-step action flows for each onboarding responsibility. Click any row to expand.

1

Screen New Candidates (Indeed)

📌 New candidate applications available in Indeed under "New" status.
👤 Brenda, Paula, Natalia 🕐 Multiple times/month as needed
▼
1) Open Indeed → Jobs → Select job post → Focus on New applicants (oldest → newest). 2) Verify candidate is within 1 hour of a major airport or major city (confirm via Google Maps). 3) Review mandatory screener questions — candidate should ideally answer "Yes" to all. 4) Review resume: manual/hands-on work, HVAC experience, stable work history, professional maturity. 5) Check Indeed right-side panel: Status, Activity Fit, multiple applications, prior rejections. 6) If candidate does NOT meet criteria → Reject. If candidate meets criteria → Select the (?) icon next to their name.
2

Prepare & Send Consolidated Onboarding Checklists

📌 One week prior to remote orientation, after receiving Brenda's ORIENTATION email.
👤 Paula 🕐 One week before scheduled orientation
▼
Pre-step: Confirm cell phone number and mailing address via Indeed messaging. 1) Reply ALL to Brenda's ORIENTATION email. 2) Create a checklist for each new technician in the orientation group. 3) For each technician, verify and document: • Digital onboarding is complete (confirmed in New Hire Spreadsheet; if unclear, follow up with Lisa). • OSHA 10 or OSHA 30 Construction certificate is on file. • High school diploma, transcript, or GED certificate is on file. 4) Indicate clearly what is completed and what is still pending prior to Day 1. 5) Combine all individual checklists into one single email. 6) Send to Brenda and the onboarding team.
3

Email Follow-Up – Update Onboarding Status

📌 Friday morning before orientation week.
👤 Paula 🕐 Friday before orientation week
▼
1) Reply ALL to Brenda's original ORIENTATION email. 2) Update onboarding status for each technician — confirm completed items, flag any pending. 3) Include final status on: • Digital onboarding completion • OSHA 10/30 certificate on file • Diploma/GED on file • Any outstanding follow-ups with Lisa 4) This is the final status update before orientation week begins.
⚠ Final check before orientation week. Ensure all items are resolved or clearly flagged as pending.
4

Send Orientation Link & Schedule Meet & Greet

📌 Monday of Orientation week.
👤 Paula 🕐 Monday of Orientation week
▼
1) Send Orientation link to Ana for IT Onboarding: • Post the link in the Onboarding Thread in RC. • Set up a 1-hour placeholder in Outlook Calendar. 2) Set up Meet & Greet with the ATA Team: • Participants: Brian, Brenda, Techs, Paula. • Use the same Orientation link. • Check calendars — try to schedule for Wednesday of that week.
5

Add New Team Members to RC Threads & Recurring Meetings

📌 Once Brenda confirms the new technician's RC account is active in the Onboarding thread.
👤 Paula 🕐 As soon as RC account is confirmed active
▼
1) Add the new technician to all RC threads: • Company Social RC Thread • Field Tech Resources RC Thread • Training Touch Base / BZ Info Thread • Competition GROUP RC Thread 2) Create their TEAM: • Include: assigned PM + Lead Tech (Brenda) + new technician. 3) Add the new technician to all recurring meetings: • New Tech Update (Wednesdays) • TTB (Thursdays) 4) Post confirmation in the Orientation thread. Ask the new technician to confirm meeting attendance.
⚠ If the technician is a SURVEY tech, do NOT add them to the Wednesday New Tech Update meeting.
